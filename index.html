Why Vietnam Tech Society?
Regardless of numerous tech talents worldwide, the Vietnamese tech community outside of Vietnam is still considered under-represented. This can partially be attributed to the lack of an organization for Vietnamese in tech abroad, where people get together for mutual support and professional growth. To address this concern, Vietnam Tech Society (VTS) was established.
